ABOUT US

Revelation Gaming is a European Based esports team specialising in Counterstrike. We have two main aims:
1. To grow and excel in competitive counterstrike.
2. To prodive quality counterstrike content for the community.
We compete in divisions across Europe and are seeking to develop into a leading prescence in global Counterstrike. We also aim to provide quality content for the UK and European Counterstrike community, through video montages, hosted events and player articles. We are always seeking to add partners to the Revelation family so do get in contact if you would like to collaborate.
